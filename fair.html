About the Christmas Fair

üéÖ Welcome to Invictus-ville Christmas Fair!

Join us for a magical day filled with festive fun, delicious food, exciting games, and wonderful community spirit! Our Christmas Fair transforms the school into a winter wonderland where families can come together to celebrate the season. Whether you're looking to enjoy tasty treats, play entertaining games, or simply soak in the holiday atmosphere, there's something for everyone at Invictus-ville!

All proceeds from the fair go directly towards enriching our children's educational experience through special programs, equipment, and activities. Your participation and support make a real difference!
